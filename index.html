Game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

The four Beatles are below. Even the dead ones. By clicking on a Beatle you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. If you go over the target number, you lose.

The value of each Beatle is hidden from you until you click on it. {Hint: Ringo isn't necessarily the lowest value}

The value of the Beatles changes with the start of each new game.
